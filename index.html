About the Program

We believe every child deserves a place to move their body, build friendships, and feel proud of what they learn.

The Howdy Boot Line-Dancers introduce kids to simple, upbeat line dances set to country and kid-friendly music. We focus on kindness, encouragement, and celebrating effort.

Dancers learn at their own pace, with clear, step-by-step instruction and plenty of chances to repeat moves. No partner is required, and no prior dance experience is needed.
